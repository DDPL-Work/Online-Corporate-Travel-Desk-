Important Travel Rules & Guidelines

Please read carefully before travelling — traveamer.com
🪪
Valid ID & Documents
  • Carry a valid government-issued photo ID (Passport / Aadhaar / Voter ID / Driving Licence) matching your ticket name exactly.
  • International passengers must carry a valid passport with minimum 6 months validity beyond the travel date.
  • Visa documents must be obtained prior to travel. Traveamer is not responsible for denied boarding due to visa issues.
  • Children under 12 must travel with an adult; carry birth certificate if requested by airline or immigration.
⏰
Check-in & Boarding Timelines
  • International flights: Arrive at airport at least 3 hours before departure. Check-in closes 60 minutes prior to departure.
  • Domestic flights: Arrive at airport at least 2 hours before departure. Check-in closes 45 minutes prior to departure.
  • Web check-in opens 24–48 hours before departure and closes 2 hours before scheduled departure.
  • Boarding gate closes 20 minutes before scheduled departure. Late arrivals may be denied boarding without refund.
♿
Special Assistance & Medical
  • Passengers requiring wheelchair assistance or special meals must inform the airline at least 48 hours before departure.
  • Pregnant passengers beyond 28 weeks must carry a medical fitness certificate. Most airlines restrict travel beyond 36 weeks.
  • Unaccompanied minors (ages 5–12) must be pre-registered with the airline. Additional service fee applies.
  • Notify the airline in advance for carriage of medical equipment, oxygen cylinders, or mobility aids.
🔒
Airport Security Guidelines
  • All passengers and hand baggage are subject to security screening. Cooperate fully with security personnel at all times.
  • Remove laptops, tablets, and large electronics from bags at the X-ray security checkpoint.
  • Footwear may need to be removed during screening at some airports. Wear easily removable shoes for convenience.
  • Making false statements or jokes about security threats is a criminal offence punishable by law.
